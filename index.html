CLASSROOM ARMOR ™

A complete line of defensive attack products designed to protect students from violent attacks.

EVERY CLASSROOM A SAFE-ROOM

PERCEPTION:
SCHOOL IS A SAFE PLACE, A REFUGE
REALITY:
SCHOOLS ARE HIGHLY VULERNABLE TO MALICIOUS ATTACKS

It’s a fact that most bad guys go for the “easy” targets, the innocent and defenseless, those who have little or no protection from gunﬁre, explosions, and other physical harm. Unfortunately more and more schools are becoming targets of such violence.

But a school shouldn’t look like a fortress. By nature, schools are designed for aesthetics; students learn better when they are comfortable in their surroundings. Yet these characteristics also make schools highly vulnerable to attacks.

Classroom Armor™ is designed to complement the decor of the classroom itself. In most instances our armor is invisible; but when a violent incident occurs, Classroom Armor™ can mean the difference between life and death.

SLIDING DOOR

The Sliding Barrier is a ballistic rated barrier-of-entry that can be installed in most any doorway to prevent outside access. Speciﬁcally tailored for schools, the Sliding Barrier is designed to fortify classroom door openings instantly, making the classroom a safe-room. Once deployed the teacher and students are shielded against any visual or physical contact from outside the classroom, giving law enforcement time to respond to the threat.

  • Closes & locks in one simple action
  • Eliminates visual & physical contact with the threat
  • Optional surface coverings (cork, whiteboard, etc.)
  • N.I.J. Level IIIA rated to withstand multiple blasts from handguns & shotguns
  • Withstands sledge hammer & axe breaching attempts

DOOR ARMOR KIT

The Door Armor Kit (patents pending) allows you to easily convert most any existing door into a ballistic barrier-of-entry. The kit includes all of the materials and hardware necessary to retroﬁt an existing door, including latch einforcing hardware. Designed for schools, the Door Armor Kit fortiﬁes classroom door openings instantly, making each classroom a safe-room. Once deployed the teacher and students are shielded against physical threats coming from outside the door.

  • Custom ﬁt to your existing door
  • Installs in less than two hours
  • Window and frame are replaced with ballistic glazing
  • Suitable for use with existing panic hardware
  • Optional surface coverings (cork, whiteboard, etc.)
  • Withstands multiple blasts from handguns and shotguns
  • Withstands sledge hammer & axe breaching attempts
